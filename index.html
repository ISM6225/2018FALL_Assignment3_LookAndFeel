Welcome to College Search!

What is this about?

Our website enables Students who want to apply to colleges to find information on Colleges and Universities in the U.S. and shortlist them. Students just need to register on our website. They can search for a colleges based on a city name and get a list of colleges in that city along with other information such as city, zip, accreditor, campuses and tuition fee. Based on this information, they can shortlist the ones they are interested in. We provide website links for these shortlisted institutions which the students can access and apply from there. One student can shortlist many institutions.
